The Dallas Mavericks had one of their best seasons in franchise history. Led by superstar players Luka Doncic and Kyrie Irving. Following a disappointing end to the 2022 - 2023 season after trading for disgruntled Kyrie Irving from Brooklyn, the Mavericks were the 8 seed at the trade deadline. Owner Mark Cuban made a trade for PJ Washington and Daniel Gafford, and the Mavericks made the NBA Finals as a 5 seed, losing to the Boston Celtics in 5 games.
